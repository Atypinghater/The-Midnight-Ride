Frequently-Asked Questions

Q: Why should I not use the High Resolution Texture Pack DLC?

A: The texture pack DLC provides a very marginal visual increase for a potentially major performance decrease. It is simply not worth the trade off

Q: Why are there no big visual mods in the guide? Can I add my own?

SA: The simple answer is because I don't use texture mods. I don't believe they are necessary and I prefer the consistent performance and visual style without them. Fallout 4 also already has a great weather system that works perfectly. Interior lighting mods also usually damage performance due to the new lighting effects and dynamic shadows they may add (Interiors Enhanced does not damage performance, as it does not add shadows/lighting effects). It is only recommended to add your own mods if you are an experienced modder. If you are an experienced modder, I would recommend using the texture optimization step from BiRaitBec's guide (but don't use the Quality addon or any other parts of the guide).

Can I safely add Sim Settlements 2 to the guide?

A: Yes, but it needs a few minor tweaks. First, make sure you grab all the mod's requirements. Second, grab the SS2 - XDI Patch. And finally, re-run the Ruddy88's Simple Sorter steps from the Final Steps page using this replacement script instead of the original. The script has been kindly provided by Nexus user IDontEvenKnow. Details can be found in their pinned comment on the Ruddy88's Simple Sorter page.

Q: I've changed a hotkey, but the HUD still shows the previous hotkey! How can I fix this?

A: Open up Interface/DEF_CONF/DEF_INV.xml from Ruddy88's Simple Sorter. From lines 14-27, you can change the HUD to show the new hotkey. For example, if you changed the activate key from E to F, change <E>E</E> to <E>F</E>

Why does the guide not use BethINI or clean the DLC masters?

Cleaning DLC masters is not necessary, and it won't lead to any performance/stability gains. BethINI makes many undocumented/untested INI changes that can be placebo at best and dangerous at worse. Your game will be perfectly stable and performance-friendly with this guide.

Q: When installing multiple mods from the same page through MO2, should I select Merge, Rename, or Replace?

A: Read the Mod Installation Advice section from the MO2 page.

Q: Why do I get a Application Load Error 5:0000065434 message when I try to launch the game?

A: Run your game directly through Steam once. You may also need to restart Steam and/or Mod Organizer 2. If that still doesn't work, place a copy of your Steam.exe (a copy, not a shortcut) into the game's Root folder.
