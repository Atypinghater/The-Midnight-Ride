The Goal of the Guide:

This guide will walk you through how to install Fallout 4 and many essential mods to improve stability and gameplay/balance. There won't be any major content mods or drastic gameplay/texture overhauls, but the game should still feel fresh even if you have played the game many times before. This was designed with survival mode in mind, but should be fine on any other difficulty as well.

Requirements:

  • An English copy of Fallout 4 with ALL of the DLCs (but NOT the High Resolution Texture Pack DLC)
    • Only the English version of the game is supported for maximum compatibility
  • Windows 7 or higher (64bit)
  • A text editor (I recommend Notepad++, but default Windows notepad will work)
  • An archiving tool (I recommend 7-Zip)
  • Latest GPU drivers (Either NVIDIA or AMD)
  • At least 37 gigabytes of free drive space
  • A Nexus Mods account
